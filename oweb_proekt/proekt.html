    In a cast iron skillet, heat 2 tbsp of avocado oil. Season the chicken with salt and pepper on both sides. Cook the chicken in the skillet until completely cooked, flipping halfway. Remove from the heat when the internal temperature reaches 165°F. While the chicken is cooking, mix the bread cubes in a bowl with avocado oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. Pour the tossed bread onto the parchment paper lined baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Toss halfway through. Then, to make the dressing, whisk all of the ingredients together in a bowl. Taste and adjust to your seasoning needs. Prep the salad by adding the lettuce to a big bowl and tossing with 2/3 of the dressing.
